Medussa Studio

Aug 23, 2022 · Amir Orbe

This project arises from an idea I had when I saw how people still use paper to register appointments and usually do not keep track of it.

So I decided to implement a system in which there are administrator users and clients. Clients can schedule a single new appointment or for several clients that require the use of a service by selecting the date in a calendar and after selecting the date you can also select a service that The company offers for a specific time and depending on the appointments you want to register you can select the service for each registration, the system will calculate the time that the service will last for each registration. With these appointments the client can modify or cancel and generate new ones with certain conditions. After registering the appointments, the administrator has to approve the appointment, reject it or postpone it, for the administrator’s view, he can select the date he wants to see his appointments registered by his clients and can modify the status of each registration.

The administrator can also download summary dynamic excel files selecting the start date and end date in which you want to calculate the appointments you had and the income from each appointment. The main idea is to make life easier for any entrepreneur, whether is big, medium or small.
